1. Introduction

This Insurance Policy explains the available coverage, exclusions, and your responsibilities when renting a vehicle from our platform. By renting a vehicle, you agree to the terms and conditions outlined below.

2. Insurance Coverage Options

  • Collision Damage Waiver (CDW): Reduces your financial liability for damage to the rental vehicle. A deductible may apply.
  • Liability Insurance: Covers third-party injuries and property damage. Minimum coverage complies with local regulations.
  • Personal Accident Insurance (PAI): Covers medical expenses for the renter and passengers in case of an accident.
  • Theft Protection (TP): Covers loss if the vehicle is stolen. Does not include personal belongings inside the vehicle.

4. Renter Responsibilities

  • Report any damage, accidents, or theft within 24 hours.
  • Operate the vehicle responsibly and follow all traffic laws.
  • Pay for any parking tickets, tolls, or violations.
  • Ensure only authorized individuals drive the vehicle.

5. Additional Insurance

You may decline our insurance if you are covered through a personal auto policy or a credit card that includes rental coverage. You must provide valid proof at the time of rental.
