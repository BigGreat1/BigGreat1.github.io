Frequently Asked Questions

1. What types of cars do you sell?

We offer a wide variety of vehicles including sedans, SUVs, trucks, and electric vehicles from both new and used inventories.

2. Can I apply for car financing online?

Yes! You can apply for financing directly on our website. Simply visit our Finance page and fill out the secure form.

3. Do you accept trade-ins?

Absolutely. Bring your current vehicle and we’ll give you a fair trade-in offer based on its condition and market value.

4. What warranty do your cars come with?

All our vehicles come with either a manufacturer’s warranty or our dealership-backed warranty depending on the vehicle’s age and mileage.

5. Can I book a test drive online?

Yes, you can schedule a test drive using our online form. Just select the vehicle you’re interested in and choose a date and time that works for you.
